electric jetta

2008-2011

Electric car conversion of a VW Jetta. Goes about 25 miles/charge with a top speed of 55mph. The car was dismantled in 2011 after having multiple mechanical issues.

Major Features:

Curtis controller, Warp 9 motor, 12 Trojan T-1275 (144v), Battery watering system, PakTrakr battery monitor, Quick Charge, Power brakes
